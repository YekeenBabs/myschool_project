APC S.L.G Saturday 22 0ct. 2022.

All Hail the Honourable Minister for Works and Housing; We celebrate another Surulerian; the Honourable Minister for Works and Housing, Mr. Babatunde Raji Fashola (SAN), a recipient of the Nigeria Excellence Award in Public Service (NEAPS). Mr. Babatunde Fashola has committed his life for over 18 years to public service, having served as a Chief of Staff to the Governor of Lagos State, two terms as Governor of Lagos State and two terms serving Minister of the Federal Republic of Nigeria. The successes recorded in the various public capacities are visible and the most recent of them is the completion of the Second Niger Bridge in Anambra State. As a Surulerian, we are proud of you and we pray to the Almighty to continue to strengthen you and grant you more successes in office. Congratulations Your Excellency, the Honourable Minister. e-signed: Fuad Kayode Laguda Chairman, All Progressives Congress (APC), Surulere
Mr. Fuad Kayode Laguda

Gbajabiamila: Parliamentary Excellence Award Well Deserved; As President Muhammadu Buhari confers the award for Parliamentary Excellence to the Speaker, Federal House of Representatives, Rt. Hon. Speaker Femi Gbajabiamila, we join other Nigerians to celebrate yet another attestation to the good work of Mr. Speaker. Since joining the House and rising through the various ranks, Rt. Hon. Femi Gbajabiamila has been a distinguished representative with excellent leadership skills, service to humanity and an apt administrator, whose attributes have given the House a new look. The Nigeria Excellence Award in Public Service (NEAPS) Award for Rt. Hon. Gbjabiamila is well deserved and would spur him to continue the good work, not just for the people of Surulere but also for Lagos State and Nigeria as a whole. Congratulations Mr. Speaker. e-signed: Fuad Kayode Laguda Chairman, All Progressives Congress (APC), Surulere
Mr. Fuad Kayode Laguda
